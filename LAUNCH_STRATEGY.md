# Community & Launch Strategy

This document provides a high-level strategy for building the CritterCraft community, distributing the native token, and executing a phased mainnet launch.

*(Note: This is a placeholder file and will be expanded with detailed specifications.)*

## Phase 1: Community Building

*   **Content Marketing:** Articles, blog posts, and social media content explaining the project's vision and technology.
*   **Discord & Telegram:** Fostering a vibrant community through active engagement and moderation on key platforms.
*   **Ambassador Program:** Empowering community members to become advocates for the project.

## Phase 2: Conceptual Token Distribution

*   **Airdrops & Incentives:** Rewarding early supporters and testnet participants.
*   **Public Sale:** A well-regulated public sale to distribute the token to a wider audience.
*   **Ecosystem Fund:** A portion of the token supply will be reserved for grants, partnerships, and future development.

## Phase 3: Phased Mainnet Launch

1.  **Testnet v1 (Internal):** Initial, feature-incomplete network for internal testing.
2.  **Testnet v2 (Incentivized):** Public testnet with rewards for active participation and bug hunting.
3.  **Mainnet (PoA):** Initial mainnet launch with a permissioned set of validators for stability.
4.  **Mainnet (Decentralized):** Transition to a community-governed, decentralized network via NPoS/DPoS.

## Phase 4: Post-Launch Marketing

*   **Partnerships:** Collaborating with other projects in the Polkadot ecosystem and the wider crypto space.
*   **Press & PR:** Engaging with media outlets to raise awareness.
*   **Ongoing Community Engagement:** Continuing to support and grow the community through events, contests, and open communication.
