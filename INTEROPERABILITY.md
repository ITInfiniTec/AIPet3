# Interoperability Plan

This document describes the plans for ensuring CritterCraft can communicate and interact with other blockchain ecosystems.

*(Note: This is a placeholder file and will be expanded with detailed technical specifications.)*

## Polkadot Ecosystem (Native)

*   **Cross-Consensus Message Passing (XCM):** As a parachain, CritterCraft will use XCM as its primary method for interoperability.
    *   **Use Cases:**
        *   Transferring assets (e.g., DOT, KSM, stablecoins) to and from the CritterChain.
        *   Interacting with DeFi protocols on other parachains.
        *   Allowing NFTs from other chains to be used within the CritterCraft universe (e.g., as special items or character skins).

## External Chains (Bridged)

*   **Bridge to Ethereum:** A bridge will be developed or integrated to connect CritterChain to the Ethereum ecosystem.
    *   **Bridge Type:** Research will be conducted to determine the best bridge solution (e.g., Snowbridge, a third-party provider).
    *   **Use Cases:**
        *   Allowing Ethereum-based assets (ERC-20, ERC-721) to be brought into CritterCraft.
        *   Enabling CritterCraft NFTs to be traded on major Ethereum marketplaces like OpenSea.
