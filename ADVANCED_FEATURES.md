## Planned Features

*   **Pet Breeding:** Mechanics for combining Critter attributes to create new, unique offspring.
*   **Pet Day Cares:** A system for players to stake their Critters to earn rewards or train them passively.
*   **User Shops:** Player-owned storefronts for trading NFTs and in-game items.
*   **Advanced Jobs:** Complex, multi-step jobs for Critters that require specific skills or attributes.
*   **Treasure Hunts:** On-chain and off-chain puzzles and challenges that lead to rare rewards.
*   **Mini-Games:** A collection of simple, engaging games that allow players to earn in-game currency.
