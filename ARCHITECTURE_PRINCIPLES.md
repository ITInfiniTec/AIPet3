# Architectural Principles

This document outlines the core philosophies guiding the development and evolution of CritterCraft's architecture.

*(Note: This is a placeholder file and will be expanded with detailed specifications.)*

## Core Tenets

*   **Modularity:** The system will be designed in distinct, independent modules (pallets) to ensure separation of concerns and facilitate upgrades.
*   **Security:** A security-first mindset will be applied at all levels of development, from smart contract design to frontend implementation.
*   **Scalability:** The architecture will be designed to handle a growing user base and increasing on-chain activity, with a clear path for layer-2 solutions or parachain integration.
*   **Decentralization:** The project will progressively decentralize, empowering the community to take ownership of the network and its future.
*   **User-Centricity:** The end-user experience is paramount. The technology should serve the gameplay and economic loops in a way that is seamless and intuitive.
